About us

Sand Studio, a product team from Tongbu Networks, was started in 2011. The seed of AirDroid was planted when we became tired of looking for USB cables and installing drivers. Since the first release on Google Play in November 2011, AirDroid has gained more than 20 million downloads and a steady average user rating of 4.7+ stars. It’s our mission to make AirDroid more useful, powerful, and also beautiful.
